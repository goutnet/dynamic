Directions for use:

----------------------------------------
Weights go in in:
..\echonet\dynamic\weights

----------------------------------------
Videos go in: 
..\echonet\dynamic\a4c-video-dir\Videos

---------------------------------------
FileList.csv goes in:
..\echonet\dynamic\a4c-video-dir\
Should include columns for:
"FileName", "EF", "ESV", "EDV", "FrameHeight", "FrameWidth", "FPS", "NumberOfFrames", "Split"
** EF, ESV, EDV probably not required
    

---------------------------------------
Command to run:
echonet segmentation --save_video --dont_train --load_weights weights/deeplabv3_resnet50_random_best.pt
